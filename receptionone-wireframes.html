User flows

How people move through it

Three journeys: the patient booking by chat, staff running the day, and the system sending notifications.

1 · Patient booking (chat)

Patient-initiated, so the conversation is free. Covers book, reschedule, cancel, symptom routing, and the emergency exit.

flowchart TD
  A(["Patient messages the clinic"]):::entry --> B{"First contact?"}
  B -- "Yes" --> C["Ask consent to message"]:::sys
  B -- "No" --> D["Greeting + menu"]
  C --> D
  D --> E{"What do they want?"}
  E -- "Book" --> F["Choose doctor or specialty"]
  E -- "Reschedule" --> R["Find their booking"]
  E -- "Cancel" --> X["Find their booking"]
  E -- "Describe symptoms" --> S["Route to department + flag urgency"]:::ai
  S --> SE{"Red flag?"}
  SE -- "Yes" --> SU[/"Urge immediate care"/]:::warn
  SE -- "No" --> F
  F --> G["Show open slots"]
  R --> G
  G --> H{"Pick a slot"}
  H -- "None suitable" --> G
  H -- "Picks one" --> I["Confirm details"]
  I --> J(["Booked — confirmation sent"]):::done
  X --> XC(["Cancelled — slot freed"]):::done
  J --> K[/"Reminders sent 24h and 2h before"/]:::sys
  classDef entry fill:#0E7C6B,stroke:#073B33,color:#fff;
  classDef done fill:#0A4339,stroke:#062B24,color:#fff;
  classDef warn fill:#EDA23B,stroke:#b9781f,color:#3a2606;
  classDef ai fill:#F8E7CD,stroke:#EDA23B,color:#5a4112;
  classDef sys fill:#EAF1EF,stroke:#BBDDD2,color:#0B2722;

2 · Staff running the day

Reception and admins manage everything from the dashboard.

flowchart TD
  L(["Staff opens dashboard"]):::entry --> M["Sign in"]
  M --> N["Day view — today's appointments"]
  N --> O{"Action"}
  O -- "New appointment" --> P["Search patient by phone"]
  P --> Q{"Found?"}
  Q -- "Yes" --> T["Pick doctor, then slot"]
  Q -- "No" --> P2["Capture name"]
  P2 --> T
  T --> U(["Booked"]):::done
  O -- "Reschedule" --> RS["Pick a new slot"]
  RS --> RU(["Updated"]):::done
  O -- "Cancel" --> CC(["Cancelled"]):::done
  O -- "Manage doctors" --> AD["Edit doctors and weekly hours"]
  classDef entry fill:#0E7C6B,stroke:#073B33,color:#fff;
  classDef done fill:#0A4339,stroke:#062B24,color:#fff;

3 · Notifications

Confirmations on every change, and reminders that are sent exactly once.

flowchart LR
  B1["Booking or change"] --> C1["Send confirmation"]:::sys
  T1(["Reminder job runs"]):::entry --> F1{"Due in 24h or 2h?"}
  F1 -- "No" --> W1["Wait"]
  F1 -- "Yes" --> I1{"Already reminded?"}
  I1 -- "Yes" --> SK1["Skip"]
  I1 -- "No" --> S1(["Send reminder once"]):::done
